How I got here

For the past 17 years, I've been deeply involved in the world of medical imaging technology, which has been incredibly fulfilling, especially when it comes to helping patients. My drive and ambition have served me well in this field, especially in leadership roles where I had the opportunity to make a real difference in improving patient care and enhancing the work environment for my team. I realized that my work had honed some unique skills that aren't common in my field, like a keen eye for detail and a proactive approach to solving problems and improving processes. These skills, along with my passion for working with cutting-edge technology, inspired me to shift my career path toward Software Engineering. I'm excited by all facets of this field and have spent the last two years training to become an engineer, eager to apply my drive and determination to make a significant impact in the tech world. Now, I'm ready to dive into new challenges and see how my background and skills can contribute to innovation and success in your company. So, what can I engineer for you?
